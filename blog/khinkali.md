# Khinkali

The elephant logo with a rolling pin is nice, of course. But we also need small icon formats to use in the IDE.

In the first versions of the [IDEA plugin](https://plugins.jetbrains.com/plugin/28842-testo/), [@xepozz](https://boosty.to/xepozz) used a baker's hat ("tubeteika").

![Baker's hat](/blog/khinkali/img-1.jpg)

However, when getting closer to the identity question, without much thought, I decided to draw something different: khinkali!

I'm sure there are thousands of ways to prepare dough with meat. Khinkali is one of them.

![Khinkali](/blog/khinkali/img-2.jpg)

It looks cool and is already available in [plugin v2025.0.3](https://plugins.jetbrains.com/plugin/28842-testo/versions/stable/884750), but for us the work on khinkali doesn't end here:
